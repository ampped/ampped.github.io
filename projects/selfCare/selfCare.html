Story

In a field like design, where the only effort we can put in is time, it’s hard to admit that sometimes we need to take a break.

We get exhausted and stressed over perfecting projects and how our futures depend on our work. But it’s ok to take a break and it’s absolutely important to take care of yourself.

Right before starting this project, I attended a talk about self care for artists and designers. The talk did well in covering basic care techniques, but when it came time for the Q & A, the speaker couldn’t answer any of the questions asked. They were serious questions that probably everyone has asked from time to time, and I felt that they needed to be addressed.



After that experience, I became extremely aware of how much the people around me were stressed and doubting themselves. At the time, so many of my friends were pulling all-nighters, sometimes multiple in a row. I felt compelled to make this project to tell people this:

Writing the Script

To understand what exactly I needed to tell people, I interviewed my friends and classmates to find out what kind of doubts they have and what they need to feel better. Later that week, my interpersonal communication class happened to cover social support. I took this chance to interview my professor and learn how to better respond to the doubts I found.
